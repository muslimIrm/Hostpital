Psychology

Psychology is the study of the human mind and behavior. It helps us understand emotions, thoughts, and actions. Our hospital provides psy chological counseling and therapy to support mental well-being.

Psychiatry is a medical field that focuses on diagnosing and treating mental health disorders. Our hospital offers psychiatric evaluations, therapy, and medication management to support mental well-being.
